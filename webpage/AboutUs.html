E-Read: Digital Library and Online Social Reading

E-Read: Digital Library and Online Social Reading E-Read: Digital Library and Online Social Reading is a social reading platform that aims to connect people who interest is reading or writing a book. The website let the user to choose their favorite books and read it for later. For users who aim to be a successful writer or just want to share a story the platform is perfect for those person. The platform allows users to write and publish stories and be read by another users.
