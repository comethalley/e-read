About Us

Ribrary is an online reading platform that aims to connect people who is interested in reading and listening such as ebooks and audiobooks. The website also offers podcast for entertainment purposes. The platform allows users to upload their research and let everyone provide a feeback about it. Sit back and relax and choose Ribrary.

Mission

Our mission is to create a platform where everyone enjoy reading, listening and watching in their leisure time.

Vision

Our vision is to be number pick in digital library all across.
